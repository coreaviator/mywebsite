Frequently Asked Questions

How long does PPL training take?

Training duration varies from 40-80 hrs. Students typically complete in 65 hours, but accelerated schedules are possible with 3+ weekly flights. Expect 2-4 months.

Is training conducted in groups?

No‚Äîall instruction is one-on-one with certified instructors.

What FAA regulations govern training?

Training follows FAA Part 61 regulations, the set of regulations that governs how individual pilots & flight instructors are certified and trained in the United States.

Do I get a license at 40 hours of flight time?

Certification depends on proficiency. All students must meet FAA performance standards as evaluated by the instructor. Preparing before your training leads to quicker results.

Are payments required upfront?

No‚Äîpay per session as training progresses.

Any enrollment fees?

A $99 enrollment fee covers profile setup and administrative tasks (e.g., student certificate processing).

TSA Flight Security Program requirement? Non-citizen?

Only required for non-U.S. citizens; U.S. citizens are exempt. If you are not a US citizen, you may still be eligible to fly with certain visas and prerequisites completed before training begins.
