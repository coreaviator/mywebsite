Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to get a pilot certificate?

+

On average, $14,000. The cost of obtaining a pilot certificate varies depending on several factors, including the type of certificate, how quickly you progress through training, and how frequently you fly. The cost covers aircraft rental, instructor fees, study materials, and examination costs. We can create a customized training plan to fit your budget.

How long does it take?

+

The time required to earn your pilot certificate depends on your training frequency and how quickly you master the required skills. For a Private Pilot License, the fastest completion is within a month, flying two times a day. Most learners complete their training within 3-6 months when flying 2-3 times per week. We work with your schedule to create a training timeline that matches your availability and goals.

Do you use a curriculum?

+

Yes, we use a structured curriculum for all our training programs. Our curriculum is based on the FAA's Airman Certification Standards and is enhanced with the industries proven teaching methodologies. This ensures consistent, comprehensive training that covers all required knowledge and skills. The curriculum includes both ground school and flight training components, with clear milestones to track your progress. You will be notified about the next task in your training.

How often should I fly?

+

For optimal progress, we recommend flying at least 4 times per week. This frequency helps reinforce learning and build muscle memory, reducing the total time and cost of your training. However, we understand that personal and professional commitments may impact your availability. Our instructors will work with you to create a schedule that balances ideal learning conditions with your real-world constraints.

Will I be assigned a flight instructor?

+

Yes, you will be assigned a primary flight instructor who will be responsible for overseeing your training from start to finish. This ensures consistency in your instruction and allows your instructor to become familiar with your learning style and needs. In the event your primary instructor is unavailable, we have other qualified instructors who can step in to keep your training on schedule. We carefully match students with instructors based on personality, teaching style, and scheduling compatibility.
