About Core Aviator Training

We are dedicated to transforming aviation passion into real-world skill. Based in Fort Myers, our flight school uses modern simulators and training aircraft, operated by experienced instructors.

Whether you're learning for leisure or launching a career in aviation, we support every step of your journey with structure, safety, and personal mentorship.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to get a pilot certificate?

On average, around $14,000. Costs vary depending on frequency and type of training.

How long does training take?

Private pilot training can take 1–6 months depending on your availability and progress speed.

Do you follow an FAA curriculum?

Yes. We use FAA-approved curriculum backed by modern instructional methods for consistency and quality.
