What Is A Discovery Flight?

A Discovery Flight at Core Aviator Training is an introductory flight experience that allows you to take the controls of an aircraft with a certified flight instructor by your side. It's a hands-on opportunity to learn the basics of flying, experience the excitement of piloting a plane, and see the world from a new perspective. From this first flight, the possibilities are endless—whether you’re sparking a new passion, starting your journey to becoming a licensed pilot, or enjoying a unique adventure. At Core Aviator Training , a Discovery Flight is the perfect gateway to explore the skies.
